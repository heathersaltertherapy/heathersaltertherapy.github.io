Welcome

My name is Heather Salter. I am a person-centred therapist working with individuals over 18. I offer ongoing therapeutic support to aid you in your journey to better wellbeing and personal growth. I specialise in the following difficulties:

  • Relationships & Dating
  • Low self-esteem & lack of confidence
  • Anxiety and Depression
  • Phobias
  • Sexuality, Gender, LGBTQ+ difficulties
  • ADHD & ASC (neurodiversity)
  • Grief and Loss
  • Stress management
  • Anger management
  • Personal Growth
